About Us

Cultivating Intellect & Character

The Department of Academics at Crescent Model Higher Secondary School stands as the pillar of our institution's reputation. We believe that education is not merely about grades, but about igniting curiosity and fostering critical thinking.

Our faculty comprises distinguished educators and scholars dedicated to providing a holistic learning environment. From state-of-the-art science laboratories to extensive digital libraries, we equip our students with the tools necessary to excel in a rapidly evolving world.
